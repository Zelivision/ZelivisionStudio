FAQs

How long does project execution take?
The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project, but on average, it takes 1 to 3 weeks.
What are the costs for a project?
A normal render typically costs between 20 and 50 euros, while an average 20-second animation costs around 100 to 500 euros.
Which payment methods are accepted?
I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, and credit card.
Can I make changes to my project after it has started?
Yes, I am available throughout the process to make changes and adjustments. However, please note that additional fees may apply depending on the scope of the changes.
